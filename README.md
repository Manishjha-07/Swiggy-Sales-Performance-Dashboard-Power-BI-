# Swiggy Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔗 Project Overview
This project is an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard built using Power BI.  
It analyzes Swiggy’s sales data to generate meaningful business insights and track overall performance.

---

## 📊 Overall Performance Summary

- Total Sales: 53.01M  
- Total Orders: 197K  
- Average Order Value (AOV): 268.51  
- Average Rating: 4.34  
- Estimated Profit: 10.60M  

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

### 1️⃣ Consistent Monthly Growth
Sales remain stable throughout the year with minor fluctuations.  
February shows a small dip, but performance quickly recovers, indicating steady demand.

### 2️⃣ Top Cities Driving Revenue
- Bengaluru contributes the highest sales (~5.5M).  
- Lucknow, Hyderabad, and Mumbai are also strong revenue contributors.  
A few major cities generate a significant portion of total revenue.

### 3️⃣ Category Performance
The "Recommended" category contributes more than 70% of total category sales.  
This shows that curated and suggested food options strongly influence customer purchases.

### 4️⃣ Rating Impact on Sales
Higher-rated categories tend to perform better in terms of sales.  
Customer satisfaction clearly plays an important role in revenue growth.

### 5️⃣ Profit Optimization Opportunity
With an estimated profit of 10.60M, improving low-performing cities and categories can further increase profitability.
